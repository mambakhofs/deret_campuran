🧮 Kalkulator Deret Campuran

Gunakan operator +, -, *, / dan ** untuk perpangkatan.

📚 Contoh Soal

1. 5 + 3 × 2

3 × 2 = 6

5 + 6 = 11

Jawaban = 11

2. (15 + 3) ÷ 3

15 + 3 = 18

18 ÷ 3 = 6

Jawaban = 6

3. 20 - 4 × 2 + 6

4 × 2 = 8

20 - 8 = 12

12 + 6 = 18

Jawaban = 18

4. (8 - 3) × (2 + 4)

8 - 3 = 5

2 + 4 = 6

5 × 6 = 30

Jawaban = 30

5. 2² + 10 - 3

2² = 4

4 + 10 = 14

14 - 3 = 11

Jawaban = 11
